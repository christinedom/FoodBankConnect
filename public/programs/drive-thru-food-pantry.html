About

The St. Ignatius Martyr Catholic Church operates a drive thru food pantry to serve individuals and families in need. Volunteers provide fresh produce, shelf-stable groceries, and other essentials in a convenient drive thru format to ensure quick and safe distribution. Volunteers will perform food pick-ups with partner restaurants and grocery stores. Assistance is also needed fulfilling food orders at the window. A brief Ethics form is the only requirement to participate. This volunteering opportunity follows all guidelines of the Central Texas Food Bank.

Details
  • Type: Volunteer
  • Eligibility: Open
  • Frequency: Weekly
  • Cost: Free
  • Host: St. Iganatius Martyr Catholic Church
  • Sign Up: st-ignatius.org/food-pantry
