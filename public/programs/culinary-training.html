About the Program

This hands-on job-training program is designed to teach basic culinary skills, cooking techniques, knife skills, equipment operation and professional development. Our program provides students with real-world experience in our state-of-the-art kitchen, preparing our students for a career in the food service industry upon graduation. Each session is led by culinary experts who provide hands-on training, recipe guides, and strategies. Participants also gain valuable knowledge about food safety, portion sizes, and dietary balance.
