About the Program

The Nutrition Education program helps individuals and families learn how to make healthy choices on a limited budget. Through hands-on classes and interactive workshops, participants discover practical cooking skills, grocery shopping tips, and ways to prepare affordable, nutritious meals.

Sessions are designed to be accessible for all skill levels and provide culturally relevant recipes, nutrition guidance, and interactive activities. In addition to in-person cooking classes, the Food Bank also offers online resources and virtual sessions to make healthy eating achievable for everyone.

By empowering families with the knowledge and confidence to cook balanced meals, this program supports long-term health, reduces food insecurity, and builds stronger, healthier communities across Central Texas.
