About

Dragon's Lair Comics and Fantasy originally opened in 1986 in downtown Austin. Since then, the franchise has expanded to several locations, including San Marcos and even as far as Columbus, Ohio. Dragon's Lair Comics and Fantasy is a specialty store that sells comics, games, manga, plush toys, action figures, and other pop culture collectibles.
Twice a year, Dragon's Lair hosts a semi-annual food drive called CanMander for the Hope Food Pantry, donating roughly 1,200 pounds of canned food each year. The store also partners with and supports other community organizations, including CASA, AIDS Walk Austin, and Hill Country Ride for AIDS.
