About

The Central Texas Food Bank is the largest hunger-relief charity in Central Texas, providing over 50,000 meals every week to families in need. They distribute food through partner agencies and community programs, helping fight food insecurity in the Austin region.

  • Services Offered: Emergency food assistance, nutrition education, mobile food pantry, SNAP outreach
