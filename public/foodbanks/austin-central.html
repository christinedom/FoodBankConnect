St. Ignatius Martyr Food Pantry

About

The St. Ignatius Martyr Food Pantry provides emergency food assistance to individuals and families in Austin. Operated by volunteers from the parish, the pantry serves thousands of meals monthly while fostering dignity and compassion for all who visit.

Details
  • Location: 126 W Oltorf St, Austin, TX 78704
  • Capacity: ~1,000 meals/week
  • Hours: Mon–Wed 9am–1pm, Thu 9–11am
  • Urgency: High
  • Services Offered: Groceries, pantry staples, and emergency support
