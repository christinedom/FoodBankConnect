About

The St. Ignatius Martyr Food Pantry provides emergency food assistance to individuals and families in Austin. Operated by volunteers from the parish, the pantry serves thousands of meals monthly while fostering dignity and compassion for all who visit. This pantry has been operating since 1978 and is a Gold Partner with the Central Texas Food Bank.
